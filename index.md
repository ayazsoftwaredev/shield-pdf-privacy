## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Shield PDF is an offline-first application.**

* **File Processing:** All PDF operations (including merging, splitting, encrypting, decrypting, and converting) are performed **locally on your device**. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, stored by us, or shared with third parties.
* **AI Features:** Features such as "Text to PDF" utilize your device's processing power. Input text is not sent to our servers.

## 2. Permissions Required

To provide specific features, the app may require the following permissions:

* **Storage Access:** Required to select PDF and image files from your device, process them, and save the modified files back to your storage.
* **Camera:** Required only when using "OCR Scan" or "ID Card Scan" features to capture images of documents. These images are processed locally and are not sent externally.
